# Resources

Here are great resources to learn:

1. ["These Side-by-Side Videos Will Make You Rethink Your Captioned Videos" by Meryl Evans, a great explanation about creating readable captions](https://meryl.net/why-captioned-videos-are-important/)
2. ["Captions/Subtitles" by WAI (Web Accessibility Initiative)](https://www.w3.org/WAI/media/av/captions/)
3. ["Captioning Key" by DCMP (The Described and Captioned Media Program)](https://dcmp.org/learn/captioningkey)
4. ["BBC Subtitle Guidelines" by a well-known news media, BBC](https://www.bbc.co.uk/accessibility/forproducts/guides/subtitles/)
5. ["Captions and Transcripts" by Section508.gov](https://www.section508.gov/create/captions-transcripts/)
6. ["Accessible captions: why they matter" by Piccia Neri](https://piccianeri.com/accessible-captions/)
